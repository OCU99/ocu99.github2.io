Anathema, by Twenty One Pilots

The key is E minor.

The tempo is 70 bpm.

As context, it's about having a heartache and dealing with it accordingly. What's interesting is that it never quite says why.

Key lyrics are: "I start to part two halves of my heart in the dark and I don't know where I should go and the tears and the fears begin to multiply."
